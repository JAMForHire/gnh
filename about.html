What is Gamma Nu Eta?

The purpose of Gamma Nu Eta is to recognize and promote excellence in the study and the leadership of Information Technology, to offer knowledge and services to its members' schools surrounding communities; and to play an active role in the application and evolution of Information Technology within society. Members must be strong academically and demonstrate leadership, campus involvement, and excellent character.
