What is Gamma Nu Eta?

The purpose of Gamma Nu Eta is to recognize and promote excellence in the study and the leadership of Information Technology, to offer knowledge and services to its members' schools surrounding communities; and to play an active role in the application and evolution of Information Technology within society. Members must be strong academically and demonstrate leadership, campus involvement, and excellent character.

Company Expose

There are many activities and events that Gamma Nu Eta hosts throughout the year, but the most notable one is the company expose. The Company Expose is the largest ITWS recruitment event and it features students and recruiters engaging in casual games and activities.

Newsletter

Once a year, Gamma Nu Eta sends out an email to all the RPI alumni updating them about the goings on in ITWS and RPI at large.

Tutoring

Gamma Nu Eta holds weekly tutoring sessions every Wednesday from 12PM to 1PM. You can schedule a tutoring session with them.
